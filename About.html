About Find a Film

What This Website Does

Find a Film is an interactive website designed to help users find movies based on their mood at the click of a button. We want to say goodbye to endless searching and hello to recomendations you'll love! Users simply select a mood such as happy, sad, excited, or bored, and the website generates movie suggestions instantly.

Why This Website Was Created

Choosing a movie can often be time-consuming and overwhelming because of how many options are available online. This website was created to simplify that process by linking emotions to movie genres, making it easier and faster for users to discover something they will enjoy and actually watch.

Benefits to Users

Saves time
Provides personalised suggestions
Easy and fun to use
Works across devices with responsive design

Features of the Website

This website includes multiple features such as dynamic movie loading, interactive mood selection, a contact form, and embedded multimedia content.
